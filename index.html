    Our family friendly karate club has over 40 years experience in helping people build confidence, learn self-defense and keep fit. We pride ourselves on our integral training, whilst still remebering to have fun along the way.

    Why Torashin?

    • Build confidence and feel self-assured
    • Enhance your fitness and flexibility
    • Learn self-discipline and respect
    • Ehance your focus and concentration
    • Reduce stress levels and have fun
    • Take part in tournaments and competitions
    • Within time, achieve your Black Belt

    First class instructors

    Our Instructors have spent many years refining their own training and have decades of experience of teaching adults and children alike. Our club is fully insured and all of our instructors are DBS checked.

    Regular Gradings

    Our diverse training sessions prepare our students for gradings that are held regularly throughout the year. We aim to build up each students confidence and skills, so they feel fully equipped and ready before grading.
